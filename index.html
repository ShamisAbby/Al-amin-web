About Us

This is All about Al-amin Foundation what we do and some summary of our background

Al- amin Releif Foundation

In the early 2019, Al.amin began on a small project basis, providing assistance to the poor and needed across all Tanzania and Zanzibar Islands. Through hard work, commitment, and the generous support of so many people, our operations grew over the years, and in 28th December 2020, Al.amin was officially registered with the Zanzibar government as a non profit organization and it was given a registration number Z0000090785.

From our humble waiting period we have been gotten huge achievements in our goal of helping our community.

When you give to Al.amin, you can do so with confidence, knowing that your donations will go directly to those who need it most. You can be completely assured that anything you give will go a long way.

Even the smallest donation is enough to help, save and transform someone’s life.

Over the years, we have helped provide new wells and clean drinking water to poor villages, saved hundreds of orphans from homelessness.

Today, Al.amin continues to serve the poor and destitute people of all Tanzanian and Zanzibaries Islands through our wide range of programs .

Countless people across their religions they have and continue to benefit from Al.amin’s work.

None of this would be possible without your support. Thanks to your generous donations, our work is able to continue. But the fight against poverty is far from over.

We will continue to fight the good fight, and we hope you’ll join us your support.
