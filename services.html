Installing water pumps

It is well-known that all living things on earth need clean and safe water.

It is an indisputable fact that despite the importance of water for biodiversity, the water crisis has become chronic, especially in many African countries.

And the society that suffers from water stress are children, the elderly and women who are forced to pursue these needs farther and farther away again.

In an effort to ensure that we eradicate global water scarcity, Al.amin foundation has grown to provide drilling services to the community to facilitate access to this vital service

Our foundation digs wells that are growing with national quality and standards to ensure it grows comfort in the community.

These drilling services are supported by a very large amount and financial contribution you our brother so we ask you to help our community in this by providing your support to the Al.amin foundation.

We usually drill two types of wells namely (hand wells) and (machine wells) in two completely different costs.

You join us in this to satisfy and quench the thirst of our community members.

Schools instruments

In the African societies in which we live there are economic conditions in different groups of people.

There are those whose circumstances are better where they send their children to paid schools.

And there are also those whose situation is very poor and a large group of community members, their children are sent to government schools where according to the government policy of Zanzibar education is free.

Despite the fact that education is provided free of charge by the government but many families have their children unable to go to school for various reasons including:

a) The distance from schools and community residences depending on the condition of the children is still very small.

b) Shortage of science teachers.

c) Lack of laboratory equipment for science students.

d) Lack of textbooks in school libraries

e) Lack of study materials such as handbags, pencils, shoes, and even textbooks for beginners.

f) lack of sitting benchs for many schools.

We Al.amin visit many urban and rural areas to see how we can help our communities.

We also support a wide range of equipment according to current needs and those that are most important.

Women Capital Empowering (widows)

In African societies, seeing a woman doing menial and compulsory work while having a baby is rare. This is due to the large community and women being abandoned by their husbands and giving them a very difficult time in their lives. In such a situation the Al.amin foundation does not like to see mothers experience such problems. So we decided to give them the capital they needed to start their own business. It is a group of women who are experiencing this affliction.
